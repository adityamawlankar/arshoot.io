AR Shoot

  • Augmented Reality, Programming
  • Tools: Unity, Xcode, C#
  • date: Spring 2020
  • Independant Project

I started this project brand new just like trying to learn and figure it out but intern ended up learning alot about Unity 3D.

Ideation & Strategy

I took alot of help from Unity documentation and its online resources based on that I downloded 3D assets! I then masked them on top of bottles and added properties like box collider 3D, Gravity and 3D mesh renderers. I then created a prefab out of it and multiplied it to look like bottles stacked on top of each other.

I first sat down with the client and then got to understand the things which he wanted to showcase and what his idea behind the entire process was. After understanding what problem the client had, I came with a solution to easily represnt his strategy on the brochure. I drew quick low fidelity sketches just so that he could observe the placement of things on the page. All this was beneficial in creating the prototype.

Challenge

The main challenge arrived when shooting the bottles at bottom as the bottles at top had to fall down and lie on the surface.

Solution

Had to read alot of documention but finally could solve it. Online Unity resources really helped me alot.

Reflections

This was an ambitious project as I started working on it from scratch and the only support I had was from the Unity documention online. I was able to learn alot about Unity 3D engine which would help me in future projects.
